Stickybomb Rice in PVZ2

The Stickybomb Rice is a plant in Plants vs. Zombies 2 that launches sticky bombs at zombies, causing area damage upon explosion. It is particularly effective against groups of zombies and can be strategically placed to maximize its impact.

Characteristics:

:Team Syngergies

  1. Sun Cost: 225
  2. Recharge Time: 30 seconds
  3. Damage: Area damage upon explosion
  4. Range: Medium range
  5. Special Ability: Sticky bombs stick to zombies before exploding

The Stickybomb Rice has great syngery with plants that can slow down or group zombies together, such as the Iceberg Lettuce or the Turkeypult. This allows the Stickybomb Rice to maximize its damage and stalling potential.
